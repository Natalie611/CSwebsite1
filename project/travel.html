Why Travel?

Let's start out with the facts

  1. Travel makes you heathier

    2. The study found that women who vacation at least twice a year show a significantly lower risk of suffering a heart attack than those who only travel every six years or so. The same is true for men. Men who do not take an annual vacation show a 20 percent higher risk of death and 30 percent greater risk of heart disease.

  2. Travel reduces stress
  3. Travel enhances Creativity
  4. Travel boost happiness and satisfaction
  5. Travel loweres risk of depression

Traveling is Educational and fun!

What to pack

When traveling you must be prepared because you may not find your favorite products in the place you are visiting

    1. Passport/State ID(drivers licence)
    2. Cell phone and charger
    3. Electric converters and adapters
    4. Passport/visa(s)
    5. shampoo
    6. conditioner
    7. Bathing soap
    8. mosquito replent
    9. Sunlotion
    10. Bathing suit
    11. oothbrush, toothpaste, floss, mouthwash
    12. Hair brush or comb, hair ties, barrettes/bobby pins
    13. Deodorant
    14. Headphones (consider noise-reducing headphones if you're sensitive to sound)
    15. Camera and Go Pro/video camera, memory card, and chargers
    16. Chapstick and lotion
